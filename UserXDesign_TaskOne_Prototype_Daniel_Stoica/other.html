Attraction

Taniti offers a wealth of activities for visitors seeking adventure, relaxation, and cultural enrichment.
Tourists can bask on pristine white-sand beaches or explore the vibrant coral reefs through snorkeling and diving.
For the more adventurous, the rainforest provides opportunities for hiking, zip-lining, and wildlife observation.
The island's active volcano is a must-see, offering guided tours that highlight its natural beauty and geological significance.
History and art enthusiasts can visit the local museum and art galleries, while those looking for a lively evening can enjoy the pubs and microbrewery.
Families will appreciate the arcade, bowling alley, and a soon-to-open nine-hole golf course.
With an eclectic mix of modern amenities, from helicopter tours to fishing charters to the island promises of an unforgettable getaway for every traveler.

Accommodation

Taniti provides a diverse range of accommodations to suit all tastes and budgets, making it an ideal destination for any traveler.
From charming, family-owned bed and breakfasts to a luxurious four-star beachfront resort, visitors can choose the perfect place to unwind.
For budget-conscious travelers, the island offers an affordable hostel, while those seeking a more personalized experience with lush tropical surroundings.
All lodging options on Taniti are meticulously regulated and inspected to ensure high standards of safety and comfort.
Whether you prefer waking up to ocean views or immersing yourself in the tranquility of the rainforest, Taniti's accommodations provides a perfect and peaceful stay.

Transportation

Getting around Taniti is both convenient and flexible, with a variety of transportation options tailored to visitors' needs.
Public buses operate regularly in Taniti City, running from early morning to late evening, while private buses serve other parts of the island.
For more independent travel, visitors can rent cars, motorcycles, or bicycles from local vendors, with helmets provided as required by law.
Taxis are readily available within Taniti City, and the area's flat terrain and walkable layout make it easy to explore on foot.
Most tourists arrive by air via the island's small airport, which accommodates propeller planes and small jets, though plans are underway to expand it.
Additionally, a cruise ship docks weekly at Yellow Leaf Bay, adding a touch of maritime charm to Taniti's growing accessibility.
Whether you prefer guided tours or personal exploration, Taniti's transportation system ensures a seamless travel experience.
